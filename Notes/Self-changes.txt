How it works
==================

cw-envt.py = Visual demo (1 creature, GUI, just to see the environment)
test_ga_mountain.py = Actual genetic algorithm (10 creatures per generation, no GUI, evolves over 1000 generations)
realtime_from_csv.py elite_mountain_100.csv = Visualise the creature generated.

Whats in the csv file?
Each CSV file stores the creature's DNA as a series of genes. Each gene is a row of numbers between 0 and 1 that encode the creature's body plan.
